About Chris White

Chris White is a multifaceted music producer and composer from Memphis, TN with a passion for exploring all genres of music. With over a decade of experience, he brings a unique blend of sounds to the table, from pop, hip-hop, rock, classical, blues, indie, and beyond.

As a father of three, Chris values family above all else and brings balance and dedication to his work. He's constantly pushing himself to create the best possible music.
