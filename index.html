About Chris White

Chris White is a multifaceted music producer and composer from Memphis, TN with a passion for exploring all genres of music. Born and raised in the birthplace of blues and rock 'n' roll, Chris was heavily influenced by these roots and the local hip-hop scene. With over a decade of experience in music production, Chris brings a unique blend of sounds to the table, ranging from pop, hip-hop, rock, classical, blues, indie, and beyond.

Chris got his start in music production in high school, but it wasn't until after his military service that he dove head-first into the industry. Despite balancing school with being a full-time parent, Chris found time to pursue his passion for music and develop his skills. In 2020, he made the decision to fully commit to music production and has since fallen in love with all forms of production, composition, engineering, and sound design.

As a father of three, Chris values family above all else and brings a sense of balance and dedication to his work. He is constantly pushing himself to create the best possible music, and his passion and drive are evident in every beat and chord he produces. With a diverse background and a love for all forms of music, Chris White is a talent to watch in the music industry.
