About Blue Rhino Recordings

Blue Rhino Recordings is a music production company offering a range of services including composition, production, sound design, and engineering, led by experienced producer and engineer Chris White.

Chris White is a multifaceted music producer, composer, and engineer with a passion for exploring all genres of music. With years of experience making music and a Bachelor's degree in Music Production from Full Sail University, he brings a unique blend of sounds to the table, from pop, hip-hop, rock, classical, blues, indie, and beyond.

As a father of three, Chris values family above all else and brings balance and dedication to his work. He's constantly pushing himself to create the best possible music, and his name Blue Rhino Recordings is a reflection of that, with "Blue" being the favorite color of his youngest son and "Rhino" being a nickname for his oldest son.
