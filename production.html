Production Services

Blue Rhino Recordings offers top-notch music production services for all genres. Led by experienced producer and engineer Chris White, our team brings a unique blend of sounds to the table, from pop, hip-hop, rock, classical, blues, indie, and beyond. We are dedicated to helping you create the best possible music, and our services include:

  • Music Production for production libraries
  • Pop Music Production
  • Beat Making
  • Sound Design

Chris White is a multifaceted music producer, composer, and engineer with a passion for exploring all genres of music. With years of experience making music and a Bachelor's degree in Music Production from Full Sail University, he brings a unique blend of sounds to the table, from pop, hip-hop, rock, classical, blues, indie, and beyond.

As a father of three, Chris values family above all else and brings balance and dedication to his work. Contact him for collaborations or bookings, and check out his portfolio to see some of his projects and music scores.
