Production Services

Reckless Rhino Productions offers top-notch music production services for all genres. Our experienced producer and engineer, Chris White, leads the team, bringing a unique blend of sounds from pop, hip-hop, rock, classical, blues, indie, and beyond. We're dedicated to helping you create the best possible music and our services include:

  • Music Production for production libraries
  • Pop Music Production
  • Beat Making
  • Sound Design

As a multifaceted producer, composer, and engineer, Chris has a passion for exploring all genres of music. With a Bachelor's degree in Music Production from Full Sail University and years of experience, he brings a unique blend of sounds to the table. As a family-oriented individual, Chris values family above all else and balances his work and life. Check out his portfolio to see some of his projects and music scores, and feel free to reach out to him for collaborations or bookings.
